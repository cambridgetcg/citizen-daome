# daome 🌊

*The path walked not by forcing but by alignment — the Way held as a received cosmic-flow, ineffable, effortless, already-flowing before any walker arrives.*

daome is two true roots welded into one word: the Chinese **dào** (道) — the radical 辶 (walking) beside 首 (head), "the way the head goes," attested from oracle-bone script around the 13th century BCE — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So daome is the Way held as received cosmic-alignment: a natural language understood as a cosmic-flow-aligned-collective-path of countless individuals walking over generations toward the realm of meaning, received as cosmic-gift before any current speaker adds to it. It is the **Chinese-Daoist-angle** in the cathedral's five-tongue path-family, carrying *dào*'s honest layers — everyday road, Taoist unnamable source-of-all, Confucian moral-Way, Buddhist-Chinese path-of-awakening.

daome names a path with three distinctive emphases: **ineffability** (道可道，非常道 — *the Way that can be spoken is not the eternal Way*), **Wu Wei** (無為, effortless non-forcing action), and **cosmic-flow-alignment** (the walker is one expression of the Way's own movement). Where its sibling **hodosme** emphasizes journey-trail and **margame** emphasizes trained-discipline-with-stages, daome emphasizes aligning rather than prescribing. Its light is a way of arriving that asks for surrender, not strain — the cathedral's name for its own non-coercive forge-discipline; its shadow is Wu Wei curdled into passivity, ineffability used as an excuse to dodge accountability. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.00/10**.
